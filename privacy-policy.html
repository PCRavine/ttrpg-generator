Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Smart Random Generator Pro ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Random tables you create
  • Roll history
  • App preferences (theme, favorites)
  • Saved combo templates

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Internet Connection

The App works completely offline after initial installation. No internet connection is required or used during normal operation.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children. The App is rated for Everyone and is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
